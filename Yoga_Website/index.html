What is Yoga?

India, the birthplace of yoga, has nurtured this ancient practice for over 5,000 years, offering a profound path to physical, mental, and spiritual harmony. Rooted in the wisdom of the Adiyogi, Lord Shiva, yoga transcends mere exercise—it is a way of life that unites the body, mind, and soul in perfect balance.

In recent decades, the world has increasingly turned to yoga as a powerful tool for holistic well-being. Visionary yogis like Swami Vivekananda, B.K.S. Iyengar, and Tirumalai Krishnamacharya have played a pivotal role in bringing this timeless tradition to a global audience.
